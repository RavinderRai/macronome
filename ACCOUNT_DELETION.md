# Account Deletion Request

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## How to Delete Your Account

In-app account deletion is currently being implemented. In the meantime, you can delete your Macronome account and all associated data at any time by contacting us:

- **Email:** ravi@ravinderrai.com

Please include your account email address in your request so we can locate and delete your account.

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

- Your account profile and authentication information
- All pantry items and food scans
- Meal preferences and dietary restrictions
- Recipe interactions and recommendations
- All personal data associated with your account

## Data Deletion Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at:
- Email: ravi@ravinderrai.com
- GitHub: https://github.com/ravinderrai/macronome

---

**Note:** Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you want to proceed before confirming deletion.
