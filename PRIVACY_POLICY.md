# Privacy Policy for Macronome

**Last Updated:** November 26, 2025

## Introduction

Macronome ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Access
Macronome requests access to your device's camera to allow you to scan and identify food items in your pantry. Camera images are processed locally on your device and sent to our servers only for the purpose of food recognition and meal recommendations. We do not store camera images permanently.

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Authentication credentials (managed by Clerk authentication service)

### Usage Data
We collect information about how you use the app, including:
- Food items you scan and add to your pantry
- Meal preferences and dietary restrictions
- Recipe interactions and recommendations

### API Usage
The app communicates with our backend servers to provide meal recommendations and manage your pantry data. This includes sending food item information and receiving personalized meal suggestions.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide food recognition and pantry management features
- Generate personalized meal recommendations
- Improve our services and user experience
- Communicate with you about your account and app updates

## Data Storage and Security

- Your account information is securely stored and managed by Clerk authentication service
- Pantry and preference data is stored on our secure servers
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information
- Camera images are processed temporarily and not stored permanently

## Third-Party Services

### Clerk Authentication
We use Clerk for user authentication. Clerk's privacy policy applies to authentication data: https://clerk.com/privacy

### Backend Services
Your data is processed by our backend services to provide meal recommendations and manage your pantry.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may delete your account at any time, which will result in the deletion of your personal data.

## Children's Privacy

Macronome is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Permissions Used

### Camera Permission
Macronome requires camera access to scan and identify food items. This permission is essential for the core functionality of the app. Camera images are processed for food recognition purposes only.

### Internet Permission
Required to sync your data with our servers and provide meal recommendations.

### Storage Permissions
Used to save pantry data and app preferences locally on your device.
